# Why land rights are key to helping Indigenous people

NCR rights is an animated infographic for a political organization that explores the awareness of indigenous land rights. They hope it can open up a new perspective on how indigenous facing challenges with land rights.

The infographic was animated using After Effects from the provided graphics and sound effects sourced from Freesound under CC Creative Commons 0.
